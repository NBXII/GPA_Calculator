How It Works

Using the Year-Based Calculator

  1. Select your Academic Year and the Number of Semesters for that year.
  2. Click "Set Up Year" to generate the semester blocks.
  3. For each semester, add your courses by filling in the course name, grade, and credits. Use the "+ Add Course" button to add more rows.
  4. Your semester GPA and overall CGPA are calculated in real-time as you add or edit courses. All changes are saved automatically.
  5. The "Courses Overview" table at the bottom shows all courses from all years. You can edit or delete courses from this master list, and your CGPA will update instantly.

GPA Formula

The formula used is: GPA = (Σ (Grade Point × Credit Hours)) / (Σ Credit Hours).
